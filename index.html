M.Tech Thesis

As a part of my masters thesis, I decided to explore the applicability of theorical knowledge in a real life situation. I examined the assembly line at TATA Bearings, Kharagpur for Inventory and Sustainability analysis (Dow Jones Sustainability Index). This topic was of interest to me as TATA, as a company is known to survive the market over decades and it would be interesting to look at one of its vertical critically. I planned to apply Theory of Constraints (TOC) techniques to optimize the inventory replenishment process of spares and consumables. It came to my understanding that these raw materials were least optimized over the line for they were mostly ordered in bulk and hence also consumed a lot of inventory space. I primarily used Holt-Winters model to improve throughput of the inventory management system. By the end of my study I could suggest improvements with the help of my professor and the line mananger to reduce the in-hand inventory by ~3%.
